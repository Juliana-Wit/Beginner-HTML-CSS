There are two main varieties of ballet flats, also known as ballet slippers, that all ballet dancers wear. While the overall structure of the shoe remains the same, ballet flats are typicaly made of leather or canvas materials.

Characteristics of Ballet Flats

  • They have very little structure
  • They provided little to no support for the foot
  • The materials that are used are meant to be flexible
