Characteristics of Pointe Shoes

  • They have more structure
  • They offer support to the dancers arch, ankle, and toes
  • The materials used to make them are ment to be maliable but still hold its shape

Dancers spend many years studying their technique and in strength training before they get their first pair of pointe shoes. Without this training, and the special support from the pointe shoes,dancers will be unable to safely dance on their toes.
